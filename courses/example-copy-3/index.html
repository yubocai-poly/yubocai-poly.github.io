Introduction to Algorithms (CSE103) is an initiation into the art and science of algorithms.

📚 Professors

  • Damiano Mazza

📚 Course Organization

  • 7 lectures (1.5h) and 7 Tutorials (2h) 24.5 hours in total with 2 ECTS

📚 References of textbook

  • T.H. Cormen, C.E. Leiserson, R.L. Rivest and C. Stein. Introduction to Algorithms. The MIT Press, 2009.
  • D. E. Knuth. The Art of Computer Programming, Vol. 3: Sorting and Searching. Addison Wesley, 1998.

📚 Course Arrangement

  • LEC 0: Introduction to Algorithms
  • LEC 1: Divide and Conquer, Sorting and Searching
  • LEC 2: Complexity Analysis 1
  • LEC 3: Complexity Analysis 2
  • LEC 4: Correctness of Algorithms
  • LEC 5: Dynamic Programming and Greedy Algorithms
  • LEC 6: Graph Algorithms
