Biography

Hi, welcome to Yubo Cai’s personal website. I am currently a bachelor student at Ecole Polytechnique double major in applied mathematics and computer sciences. I am currently a visiting research at HEC Montreal and University of Montreal, Department of Decision Sciences and Operations Research, working on the project of Mathematical Programming Methods for Semantic Web and Logical Inference and Robust Optimization in Job-shop problem.

Last summer, I was working on a project at INRIA on the topics of optimization of the computing algorithms of the time-fractional diffusion equation. Currently, I also worked on my personal project on Quadratization preprocessing for the reachability Problem at Lix and CNRS with Prof. Gleb Pogudin and the project is continuous on progress this summer.

I am currently looking for research project/internship in operation research. Also, I am looking for Phd position in relavant areas.
